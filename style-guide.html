Style Guide

This page shows the style for the Extinct Species Guide app.

Color Palette

Use a natural palette with forest greens and soft earth tones.

Primary: #2f5f44

Secondary: #acc7a5

Typography

Use clear headings and easy-to-read paragraph text.

Extinct Species Guide

This is regular paragraph text. Keep the body simple and easy to read while sharing facts about extinct animals and plants.

Button

Use one primary button style for key actions like opening a detail page.

Card Example

Cards group related species details together with a title, short description, and image.

Woolly Mammoth

Large Ice Age mammal that lived on cold plains and ate grasses.

Archaeopteryx

Early flying reptile with feathers from the Jurassic world.

Triceratops

Plant-eating dinosaur with a large frill and three horns.

Tip: Students can edit colors, spacing, and text to define app style.
